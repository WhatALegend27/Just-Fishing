# Shop/Merchant  
My feature for Just Fishing will be to manage the shop, so that the player can progress through the game and get better items.  

## Items
These will be the items available for purchase at the shop.  
* Rod Upgrades - Makes catching fish easier
* Bait - Makes rarer fish appear more
* Lures - Affects movement speed of the hook

## Selling  
In addition to selling items to the player, the shop also acts as the only way to get money as well. 
At the end of each day, the player will have the opportunity to visit the shop in order to sell the fish that they caught during the day.  
The amount of coins is determined by the amount of fish sold and the rarity of the fish, with rarer fish being sold for more coins.  
Coins can be used back at the shop to buy items/upgrades or spent on bills to prevent death.
